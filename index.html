1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

AutoTrack PRO allows users to manually enter information such as:

  • Vehicle details (cars, motorcycles, trucks, boats, etc.)
  • Fuel consumption records
  • Expenses and repair costs
  • Maintenance and service history
  • Notes, reminders, and dates

This data is entered voluntarily by the user and is used only to provide app functionality.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The app itself does not collect personal information such as your name, address, phone number, or precise location.

Limited technical data may be collected automatically by the Android operating system or Google Play services, such as crash logs, basic device information, and app performance data. This information is used solely to improve app stability and reliability.

2. How Information Is Used

Information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Providing core app features
  • Displaying statistics and summaries
  • Managing reminders and maintenance schedules
  • Improving app performance and reliability

AutoTrack PRO does not use data for advertising profiling or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

All data entered into AutoTrack PRO is stored locally on your device. The app does not upload user data to external servers unless a feature explicitly requires it.

We do not operate our own cloud storage for user data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

If third-party services are used (such as Google Play services for app distribution or crash reporting), they may process limited technical data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

All user-entered data remains on your device for as long as you keep the app installed.

You can delete all data at any time by:

  • Deleting records inside the app, or
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

For questions or special data requests, please contact us via the email address provided above.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children’s Privacy

AutoTrack PRO is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
