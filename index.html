1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

AutoTrack PRO lets you manually enter information so the app can track costs and maintenance. This may include:

  • Vehicle details (cars, motorcycles, trucks, boats, etc.)
  • Fuel entries (date, cost, liters/gallons, mileage/odometer, notes)
  • Repairs and maintenance records (parts, labor, costs, dates, notes)
  • Reminders and schedules (service intervals, registration/insurance expiry dates)

This data is provided voluntarily by you and is used only to deliver app functionality.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

AutoTrack PRO does not ask for your name, address, phone number, or precise location.

Like most Android apps, limited technical information may be processed by the Android platform or Google Play services, such as crash reports or basic device/app diagnostics. This information is used only to improve stability and reliability.

2. How We Use Information

We use information only to:

  • Provide core features (tracking expenses, fuel, and maintenance)
  • Show summaries and statistics you request
  • Display reminders and notifications you configure
  • Fix bugs and improve performance

We do not use your data for advertising profiling or sell your information.

3. Data Storage

User-entered data is stored locally on your device. AutoTrack PRO does not upload your records to our own servers.

If your device or third-party tools (e.g., Android backup features) create backups, that process is controlled by your device settings and the provider’s policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Third-party services used for app distribution or basic diagnostics (e.g., Google Play services) may process limited technical data under their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your records are stored on your device, they remain there until you delete them.

You can delete your data by:

  • Removing entries inside the app (if available), and/or
  • Clearing the app’s storage in Android settings, and/or
  • Uninstalling the app

If you need help, contact us at the email above.

7. Children’s Privacy

AutoTrack PRO is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
